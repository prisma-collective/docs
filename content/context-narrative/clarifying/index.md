The concepts introduced below are offered as foundational pillars to guide newcomers in understanding more of the inner workings of why and how we approach work in a certain way. Together, these pages are intended to form a viewpoint to return to, enabling their readers to guide themselves in a self-directed journey of discovery and development. In this collection of pages, there exists the following structural characteristics:

- They are ordered. 
- Yet, there are also links to other parts of the docs. 
- Key words may be defined further, with pages from the [glossary](/glossary). 

Therefore, these pages are not offered as a feast but a series of snacks, intended to complement a longer, multi-layered process of deepening engagement. Like a packet of fruit and nuts whilst on a hike, with each visit you may find in these clarifications the energy boost needed to better enjoy the walk to the next checkpoint. With recovery and recollection in mind, inviting clarity...
