# 👥 Stakeholder Map — Autofy

## 1. Caretakers

- Transport union leaders who guide and support drivers
- Elder community members who influence norms and community safety expectations
- Parents and guardians concerned about safe transportation for their families
- Vehicle owners who oversee compliance and maintenance

## 2. Institutions and Local Actors

- Local transport authorities responsible for regulations and enforcement
- Motor licensing offices managing documentation and renewals
- Police and road safety officers conducting checks and ensuring compliance
- Local government councils overseeing transportation policies
- NGOs or civic groups advocating for safer mobility systems

## 3. Emerging Leaders

- Youth tech innovators introducing digital solutions for mobility
- Community volunteers helping with awareness and education
- Startup founders building platforms that improve transportation efficiency
- Social media influencers raising public attention to safety and transport issues

## 4. Groups Affected by the Challenge

- Daily commuters who rely heavily on public transport
- Commercial drivers affected by complex or unclear documentation processes
- Small business owners who depend on mobility to run operations
- Students and youth who need reliable and affordable movement
- Vulnerable groups (elderly, disabled) who face higher safety risks
