## Problem
In Abuja's academic ecosystem, promising scientific research is systematically stifled by opaque funding pathways and a deep deficit of trust between donors and researchers. While funding exists locally and internationally, the lack of transparency prevents it from reaching innovators who solve critical food, health, and environmental challenges. Nigeria's STEM research ecosystem suffers from a structural trust crisis affecting every funding stage: opacity where donors cannot verify how funds are utilized with reports delayed, incomplete, or unverifiable; delays where bureaucratic processing timelines of 6-18 months slow experimentation, destroy momentum, and prevent researchers from accessing time-sensitive resources; and trust deficit where unverifiable outcomes cause donors to reduce funding, researchers to lose opportunities, and innovation to stall. Behind all these issues is the same core breakdown: research funding is managed in black boxes rather than transparent, accountable systems.

## MVP

### Scope
StemTrust introduces a blockchain-powered, milestone-based funding platform built around three key mechanisms:
- **Smart Lock (Blockchain Escrow):** Funding does not go into personal accounts but is locked securely in smart contracts, eliminating misallocation risks and ensuring funds are always traceable
- **Milestone-Based Release:** Researchers receive funds only when providing verifiable progress evidence (photos, data, logs, research outputs) with 75% approval vote from funder or participating community triggering next release, ensuring every disbursement is tied to real work—not paperwork or bureaucratic approvals
- **Direct Access, No Bottlenecks:** Connects donors directly to verified researchers, bypassing months of middle-layer administrative delay, accelerating research timelines and giving funders confidence through real-time proof of progress

### Cardano Integration
StemTrust leverages Cardano blockchain with MeshSDK and Aiken smart contracts for blockchain logic, milestone validation, and smart contract implementation ensuring transparency, security, auditability, and scalability for nationwide research programs. The platform uses transparent, immutable technology to bridge the trust gap, allowing direct verified funding to reach researchers and ensuring Nigerian science can stay in Nigeria. This transforms research transparency into verifiable digital evidence, disbursement delays into milestone-triggered automation, and donor distrust into transparent community-auditable processes—creating a funding ecosystem where researchers receive support fast, donors see exactly what they paid for, and innovation accelerates rather than stagnates. StemTrust turns STEM research funding from a high-risk gamble into a verifiable, data-backed investment.
