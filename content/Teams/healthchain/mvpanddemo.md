## Problem
In the communities we explored, people lack reliable, portable, and verifiable health records, leaving both residents and clinics dependent on fragile paper systems. This leads to misdiagnosis, repeated tests, and delayed emergency responses because health authorities receive fragmented or late information. Fragmented, paper-based, and siloed health-record systems create cascading risks: patients frequently lose medical records or must carry physical documents leading to repeated tests and inconsistent treatment; health records stored manually can be altered, misplaced, or falsified weakening clinical decision-making and surveillance accuracy; without consolidated or verifiable data, disease trends go undetected, outbreak signals are delayed, and national response becomes reactive instead of preventive; and patients lack control over who can access or use their health information, creating privacy and consent gaps. The challenge is the need for a trusted, low-cost way for individuals to own their health identity while enabling clinics and authorities to access accurate, real-time data.

## MVP

### Scope
HealthChain provides a secure, transparent, patient-controlled medical record ecosystem featuring:
- **Verifiable Medical Records:** Clinics and doctors upload records that are digitally stored, hashed on-chain, and independently verifiable, guaranteeing authenticity and building trust
- **Patient-Controlled Data Access:** Users determine who can view their records, when access is granted, and what information is shared, aligning with WHO principles on data privacy and patient empowerment
- **Early Disease Trend Detection:** Machine learning models analyze aggregate records to detect case increases, symptom clusters, and localized spikes supporting early interventions and public-health planning
- **Progressive Web App Accessibility:** Running as PWA ensures low data usage, offline-first capability, and accessibility across devices, improving inclusivity and adoption across communities

### Cardano Integration
HealthChain leverages Cardano blockchain to provide tamper-proof signatures for each medical file, ensuring transparency, non-repudiation, and long-term verification. The blockchain-backed integrity layer creates trustworthy records for clinical and public-health use while empowering patients with control over their information—a core WHO digital health priority. This technology, particularly decentralized identity and mobile-first systems, offers a meaningful opportunity to transform health communication, improve data reliability, and strengthen community resilience. HealthChain reduces fragmentation and improves continuity of care, enhances data integrity and security through blockchain verification, enables data-driven decision making through trend detection supporting timely interventions, and reduces system inefficiency by preventing duplication of tests and administrative burden—creating a future where every patient owns verifiable digital medical records, clinics trust the authenticity of every file, doctors access accurate history instantly, health authorities detect disease patterns early, and records cannot be falsified, lost, or manipulated.
