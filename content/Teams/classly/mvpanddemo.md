## Problem
The global e-learning market is massive yet structurally broken at its financial core. Independent educators—the backbone of the creator economy—are forced into a damaging trade-off: legacy platforms provide trust but lock creator earnings behind 30-60 day payment delays crushing cash flow and treating teachers as low-priority vendors, while informal channels provide fast cash but offer zero trust, zero quality assurance, and zero scalability for students. In our community, most online learning happens informally on WhatsApp, creating widespread issues of trust, low-quality instruction, and financial risk for both learners and tutors. Thousands of people lose money, receive unverifiable certificates, or fail to gain real skills despite paying for courses. This creates a fundamental contradiction: the teacher needs instant predictable income while the student needs a money-back safety net. Because this trust gap is never properly resolved, it suppresses transactions, increases disputes, reduces quality, and ultimately starves both sides of the market of fair value.

## MVP

### Scope
Classly is a decentralized FinTech infrastructure layer for the education creator economy featuring:
- **30/40/30 Accelerated Release Model:** 30% instant liquidity upon enrollment, 40% quality assurance at objective engagement milestones (e.g., 50% video completion), 30% final trust after refund window expires
- **NFT Certificates:** Tamper-proof blockchain-minted credentials issued upon course completion transforming completion into portable verifiable career assets
- **On-Chain Ratings:** Immutably recorded using Merkle Trees eliminating manipulation, fake reviews, and reputation attacks
- **Video Core Infrastructure:** Integrated video upload, hosting, and structured playback allowing teachers to define objective engagement milestones powering the 40% release trigger

This system replaces platform-controlled payouts with automated rules-based financial fairness.

### Cardano Integration
Classly leverages Cardano blockchain as the financial rail that fixes broken trust between students and teachers. Smart-contract-backed escrow restructures payment flows: immediate 30% upon enrollment removes legacy platform waiting periods giving teachers instant working capital, 40% at measurable engagement milestones ties payment directly to content consumption creating accountability, and 30% after refund window protects students while finalizing transactions predictably. The platform replaces platform-controlled trust with mathematical cryptographic trust through blockchain-minted NFT certificates, immutable on-chain ratings, and smart-contract-enforced financial logic. Classly positions as "The Stripe for the Education Creator Economy"—not competing with EdTech marketplaces but becoming the financial backbone that powers fair payments, enforces verifiable credentials, and standardizes trust across creator education ecosystems, delivering faster earnings and predictable income for teachers alongside trust, refund protection, and verifiable credentials for students.
