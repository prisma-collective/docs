## Caretakers

- Content creators / tutors delivering courses
- Admin staff verifying course completion and managing student progress
- Community managers moderating WhatsApp / Telegram learning groups
- Teaching assistants, bootcamp instructors, language tutors
- Local creatives offering specialized skills (design, tech, marketing)

## Institutions & Local Actors

- Universities, institutes, local training centers
- Online learning platforms, EdTech hubs
- Employers, recruiters, and HR bodies dependent on trustworthy certificates
- NGOs, youth development programs, skills-training organizations
- Incubators, accelerators supporting digital entrepreneurship

## Emerging Leaders

- Tech-savvy educators adopting digital tools and blockchain
- Top-rated tutors gaining influence in online communities
- WhatsApp group admins organizing large informal classes
- Influencers teaching niche digital skills
- Hackathon teams and innovators testing blockchain solutions

## Groups Affected by the Challenge

- Learners seeking job-relevant skills
- Freelancers and young professionals needing verified credentials
- Underemployed youth trying to upskill
- Workers reskilling or transitioning into new careers
- Rural and low-income learners priced out of formal education
