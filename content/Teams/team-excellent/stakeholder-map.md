# 🗺️ Stakeholder Map  Cardano Hackathon Team Excellent (AidChain)

**Nigerian Aid Distribution Ecosystem**

---

## Caretakers
*Community Support Networks*

**Local Community Leaders**
- Organize grassroots aid distribution
- Know who needs help most
- Trusted by community members
- Currently lack verification tools
- Want to prove aid reached intended beneficiaries

**Volunteers & Community Health Workers**
- Deliver aid on the ground
- Provide feedback on beneficiary needs
- Bridge between formal NGOs and communities
- Need transparent accountability systems
- Want recognition for their efforts

**Faith-Based Organizations**
- Run food banks, shelters, education programs
- Deep community trust
- Often bypass formal channels
- Struggle with donor reporting
- Need transparent tracking systems

---

## Primary Stakeholders
*Organizations and Institutions*

**NGOs & Charitable Organizations**
- Create aid campaigns
- Raise funds from donors
- Distribute resources to beneficiaries
- Face trust challenges from both donors and communities
- Need: Transparent fund management, verification systems, donor confidence

**Donors (Individual & Corporate)**
- Provide financial resources
- Want to see real impact
- Frustrated by lack of transparency
- Cannot verify if contributions reached beneficiaries
- Need: Real-time tracking, proof of impact, accountability

**Government Agencies**
- NEMA (National Emergency Management Agency)
- State Emergency Management Agencies
- Social welfare departments
- Distribute disaster relief and social support
- Need: Transparency, accountability, reduced corruption

**Verification Bodies**
- Independent auditors
- Community validators
- Impact assessment organizations
- Ensure aid reaches intended beneficiaries
- Need: Tools to verify delivery, transparent approval processes

---

## Emerging Leaders
*Innovation Drivers*

**Tech-Savvy Youth Advocates**
- Early adopters of blockchain solutions
- Social media influencers
- Push for transparency and accountability
- Can drive platform adoption
- Want to participate in verification

**Blockchain & Development Communities**
- Cardano ecosystem builders
- Open-source contributors
- Provide technical expertise
- Support decentralized solutions
- Want to see real-world blockchain impact

**Impact Investors & Donors**
- Seek measurable social returns
- Fund transparent, accountable initiatives
- Demand proof of impact
- Support technology-driven solutions
- Want to scale effective interventions

---

## Groups Affected by the Challenge
*Primary Beneficiaries*

**Vulnerable Populations**
- Low-income families
- Displaced persons (IDP camps)
- Disaster-affected communities
- Rural populations with limited access
- Chronically ill patients needing medical support

**Primary Needs:**
- Timely delivery of promised aid
- Dignity in receiving support
- Clear information about eligibility
- Ability to report issues
- Predictability and reliability

**Indirect Beneficiaries:**
- Communities surrounding aid recipients
- Future generations benefiting from infrastructure improvements
- Local economies supported by aid distribution
- Healthcare systems strengthened by medical supply chains

---

## Power Dynamics

**Who Has Resources:**  
Donors, government agencies, international organizations—control funding

**Who Has Information:**  
NGOs and community leaders—know who needs help and where

**Who Has Trust:**  
Faith-based organizations and local leaders—community credibility

**Who Lacks Power:**  
Beneficiaries—dependent on others, no verification capability, limited voice

**Who Can Bridge Gaps:**  
AidChain platform—creates transparency layer connecting all stakeholders

---

## Ecosystem Interconnections

### Donors → NGOs:
- Provide funds
- Demand accountability
- Need transparency

### NGOs → Beneficiaries:
- Distribute aid
- Need trust
- Must prove delivery

### Verifiers → System:
- Ensure accountability
- Approve disbursements
- Maintain integrity

### Community Leaders → Beneficiaries:
- Identify needs
- Facilitate distribution
- Provide local trust

### AidChain → All Stakeholders:
- Provides transparency
- Enables verification
- Builds trust
- Tracks impact
