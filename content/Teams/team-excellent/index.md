## Project Overview

### Problem Statement

In many Nigerian communities, a significant portion of aid and donations never reaches the intended beneficiaries due to corruption, lack of transparency, and fragmented record-keeping. This leads to mistrust between donors, NGOs, and the people in need, leaving vulnerable populations underserved.

The emerging challenge is ensuring that aid flows are traceable, verifiable, and accountable, so that every contribution has real impact. There is an opportunity to leverage blockchain and digital identity technologies to provide transparency, allow real-time tracking of funds, and ensure that support reaches those who need it most.

### Solution Summary

**AidChain** is a blockchain-powered aid distribution platform built on Cardano that creates transparency, traceability, and accountability throughout the entire donation and distribution process.

By leveraging blockchain technology, AidChain offers unparalleled transparency and traceability, addressing the core need for verifiable accountability in aid distribution.

#### Core Features:

**Transparent Campaign Management**
- NGOs create verifiable campaigns on-chain
- All campaign details permanently recorded
- Real-time tracking of campaign status
- Public visibility of funding goals and progress

**Donor Confidence**
- View exactly where contributions go
- Track funds from donation to disbursement
- Verify impact with on-chain evidence
- Receive NFT certificates for contributions

**Smart Contract Fund Locking**
- Donations locked in secure smart contracts
- Funds cannot be moved without verification
- Multi-signature approval for disbursements
- Protection against misappropriation

**Verification & Proof System**
- NGOs submit evidence of aid delivery
- Verifiers approve based on on-chain proof
- Transparent approval/rejection process
- Permanent audit trail of all decisions

**Automated Accountability**
- Smart contracts enforce business logic
- Milestone-based fund release
- Automated refunds for failed campaigns
- Immutable record of all transactions

**NFT Impact Certificates**
- Donors receive blockchain-verified certificates
- Proof of contribution permanently stored
- Tradeable recognition of social impact
- Community leaderboards and recognition

## How It Works

### For Donors:

1. Browse verified campaigns
2. Contribute to campaigns directly
3. Funds locked in smart contracts
4. Track campaign progress in real-time
5. Receive NFT certificate upon completion
6. View verifiable impact evidence

### For NGOs:

1. Create campaign with goals and beneficiaries
2. Submit to verification process
3. Receive donations into locked smart contract
4. Deliver aid to beneficiaries
5. Submit proof of delivery (photos, receipts, GPS)
6. Request fund release upon verification approval
7. Disburse funds with full transparency

### For Verifiers:

1. Review campaign submissions
2. Evaluate evidence of aid delivery
3. Approve or reject verification requests
4. All decisions recorded on blockchain
5. Maintain system trust and integrity

### For Beneficiaries:

1. Know aid is coming and when
2. Receive timely assistance
3. Report issues or concerns
4. Participate in feedback loops
5. Dignity preserved through transparent process

## API Endpoints

### Public:
- `GET /` - List all campaigns
- `GET /:id` - Campaign details

### Authenticated Actions:
- `POST /` - Create campaign (NGO role)
- `POST /:id/contribute` - Make contribution
- `POST /:id/lock` - Lock campaign funds (NGO role)
- `POST /:id/verify` - Submit verification evidence (NGO role)
- `POST /:id/approve-verification` - Approve campaign (Verifier role)
- `POST /:id/reject-verification` - Reject campaign (Verifier role)
- `POST /:id/mint-nft` - Mint impact certificate (Admin role)
- `POST /:id/disburse` - Disburse funds (NGO role)
- `POST /:id/refund` - Refund contributions (Admin/NGO role)
