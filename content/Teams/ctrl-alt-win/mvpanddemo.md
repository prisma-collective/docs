## Problem
In Abuja, many residents face constant uncertainty about whether the medicines they buy are genuine, as counterfeit drugs circulate through unregulated vendors and opaque supply chains. This affects health outcomes, increases treatment failure, and erodes trust in the healthcare system. Counterfeit and substandard medicines circulate daily through opaque supply chains, unreliable distributors, and unregulated street vendors. Patients cannot confidently verify if their drugs are genuine, pharmacists struggle to trace the true source of medicine batches, doctors have no secure way to enforce prescription-only access, and the result is treatment failure, drug resistance, preventable deaths, and public distrust in healthcare. This is not just a logistics problem—it is a life-and-death trust failure inside Nigeria's medicine ecosystem where fake drugs thrive where verification is weak, street vending undercuts regulated pharmacies, drug resistance increases national health costs, and public trust in healthcare continues to erode.

## MVP

### Scope
MediSure is a Cardano-powered medicine verification and prescription management system designed to restore trust, safety, and transparency in drug distribution featuring:
- **For Patients:** Receive medicine with unique QR code, scan using smartphone to instantly verify authenticity on blockchain, view complete supply chain journey, and report suspicious batches
- **For Pharmacies:** Register verified medicine batches, update blockchain as drugs move through supply chain, scan incoming stock to verify origin, issue digital prescriptions for controlled substances, and track inventory with blockchain transparency
- **For Doctors:** Issue secure digital prescriptions, ensure prescription-only drugs reach intended patients, track medication effectiveness through verified supply, and reduce prescription fraud
- **For Manufacturers:** Mint unique blockchain identities for each batch, generate tamper-proof QR codes, track products through entire distribution chain, protect brand reputation, and identify counterfeit entry points

Key features include medicine batches minted as unique on-chain assets on Cardano, QR codes generated for each batch linking to blockchain record, pharmacies and distributors updating blockchain as drugs move through supply chain, patients scanning QR codes to instantly verify authenticity, doctors issuing secure digital prescriptions ensuring controlled access to sensitive drugs, and complete supply chain transparency from manufacturer to patient.

### Cardano Integration
MediSure leverages Cardano blockchain to make every step from manufacturer to patient immutable, verifiable, and tamper-proof. The platform creates reliable, simple verification of medicine authenticity and traces origins through blockchain technology that provides transparency, improves verification, and protects patients from harmful fake medications. This enables patients to instantly verify medicine authenticity with confidence in drug safety and effectiveness, pharmacists to verify incoming stock authenticity and build customer trust through transparency while reducing liability from fake drugs, doctors to ensure prescriptions reach intended patients and reduce treatment failures from fake drugs while controlling prescription-only substance access, and the healthcare system to reduce drug resistance from counterfeit antibiotics, improve public health outcomes, restore trust in healthcare providers, and create accountability throughout supply chain—creating a future where every patient can verify their medicine is genuine with a simple scan, pharmacists protect customers through blockchain-verified supply chains, doctors know their prescriptions reach intended patients, counterfeit drugs are identified and stopped before causing harm, and trust in Nigeria's healthcare system is restored.
