## Project Overview

### Problem Statement

In Abuja, many residents face constant uncertainty about whether the medicines they buy are genuine, as counterfeit drugs circulate through unregulated vendors and opaque supply chains. This affects health outcomes, increases treatment failure, and erodes trust in the healthcare system.

Counterfeit and substandard medicines circulate daily through opaque supply chains, unreliable distributors, and unregulated street vendors. From our community exploration, we discovered that:

- **Patients cannot confidently verify** if their drugs are genuine
- **Pharmacists struggle to trace** the true source of medicine batches
- **Doctors have no secure way** to enforce prescription-only access
- The result is **treatment failure, drug resistance, preventable deaths, and public distrust** in healthcare

This is not just a logistics problem—it is a **life-and-death trust failure** inside Nigeria's medicine ecosystem.

Our community observations show a clear need for a reliable, simple way to verify medicine authenticity and trace its origins. Technology can play a meaningful role by creating transparency, improving verification, and protecting patients from harmful fake medications.

### Solution Summary

**MediSure** is a Cardano-powered medicine verification and prescription management system designed to restore trust, safety, and transparency in drug distribution.

#### How it works:

**For Patients:**
1. Receive medicine with unique QR code
2. Scan QR code using smartphone
3. Instantly verify authenticity on blockchain
4. View complete supply chain journey
5. Report suspicious batches

**For Pharmacies:**
1. Register verified medicine batches
2. Update blockchain as drugs move through supply chain
3. Scan incoming stock to verify origin
4. Issue digital prescriptions for controlled substances
5. Track inventory with blockchain transparency

**For Doctors:**
1. Issue secure digital prescriptions
2. Ensure prescription-only drugs reach intended patients
3. Track medication effectiveness through verified supply
4. Reduce prescription fraud

**For Manufacturers:**
1. Mint unique blockchain identities for each batch
2. Generate tamper-proof QR codes
3. Track products through entire distribution chain
4. Protect brand reputation
5. Identify counterfeit entry points

#### Key Features:

- **Medicine batches minted as unique on-chain assets** on Cardano
- **QR codes generated for each batch** linking to blockchain record
- **Pharmacies and distributors update blockchain** as drugs move through supply chain
- **Patients scan QR codes** to instantly verify authenticity
- **Doctors issue secure digital prescriptions** ensuring controlled access to sensitive drugs
- **Complete supply chain transparency** from manufacturer to patient

**Every step—from manufacturer to patient—becomes immutable, verifiable, and tamper-proof.**

## Target Community

**Primary Users:**

- Patients in Abuja communities (Garki, Utako, Nyanya)
- Licensed pharmacists and drug distributors
- Medical doctors and clinics
- Pharmaceutical manufacturers

**Why This Matters in the Nigerian Context:**

- Fake drugs thrive where verification is weak
- Street vending undercuts regulated pharmacies
- Drug resistance increases national health costs
- Public trust in healthcare continues to erode

MediSure directly responds to the community's demand for trust, transparency, and safety in everyday healthcare decisions.
