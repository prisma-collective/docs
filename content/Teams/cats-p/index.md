## Project Overview

### Problem Statement

**Mobile Mechanic Accessibility in Rural Areas**

Along high-traffic corridors outside central Abuja, vehicle breakdowns leave commuters and businesses exposed to significant safety risks and unnecessary economic losses due to a lack of verified, immediate repair services.

The core issue is a **critical trust gap**: digital-savvy drivers bypass closer, local roadside mechanics, opting instead for slow, expensive travel from city-based contacts, thereby wasting hours and incurring high costs for simple fixes. This disconnection prevents local mechanics from accessing a broader market while forcing motorists into vulnerable situations.

In Nigeria's rural areas, vehicle breakdowns often leave drivers stranded with limited access to reliable mobile mechanics. This causes frustrating delays, higher repair costs, and lost productivity. The lack of on-site repair services means no quick fixes, resulting in stranded vehicles, long waits, delays, and sometimes even security issues.

**Key Challenges:**

- **No platform** connects users to trusted, nearby mechanics
- **High costs** due to traveling mechanics from distant locations
- **Uncertainty and wasted time** while waiting for help
- **Safety hazards** from being stranded on dangerous roads
- **Economic losses** for drivers and businesses relying on transport

**Who is affected:**
- Commuters and travelers along Abuja's transit corridors
- Rural businesses relying on timely transport
- Local roadside mechanics with skills but no visibility
- Drivers who pay a "trust tax" to avoid using unknown mechanics

### Solution Summary

**Road_mech** is a web-based platform connecting vehicle owners with verified mobile mechanics, allowing users to book, track, and pay for services online.

We must find a way to digitize and verify the highly skilled yet currently informal roadside workforce, giving stranded drivers immediate, trusted access. We propose leveraging a web-based platform to create a verified, location-aware mobile mechanic network to drastically reduce response times and restore confidence in local service providers.

#### How It Works:

**For Drivers:**
1. Report breakdown location via web app
2. View verified mechanics nearby
3. Check ratings, reviews, and estimated arrival time
4. Book mechanic instantly
5. Track mechanic's real-time location
6. Pay securely through the platform

**For Mechanics:**
1. Register and complete verification process
2. Receive job notifications based on location
3. Accept jobs and navigate to breakdown site
4. Complete repair and receive digital payment
5. Build reputation through customer ratings

**Key Features:**

- **Verification System:** All mechanics undergo background checks and skills assessment
- **Location-Based Matching:** Connects stranded drivers with nearest available mechanics
- **Rating & Review System:** Builds trust through transparent feedback
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Drivers can see mechanic's location and ETA
- **Secure Digital Payments:** Eliminates cash transactions and payment disputes
- **Emergency Priority:** Critical breakdowns get immediate response

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:**  
To create a user-friendly platform that is accessible especially to rural drivers, connecting them instantly with verified, trusted mechanics.

**Vision:**  
To have all rural drivers' car issues tackled with the help of our platform, eliminating the "trust tax" and transforming roadside mechanics into a verified, professional service network.

## Assumptions & Constraints

**Assumptions:**
- Demand exists among rural drivers
- Decent internet connectivity in target areas
- Sufficient skilled mechanics available for verification
- Willingness to adopt digital booking system

**Constraints:**
- Data/connectivity limits in some rural areas
- Time required for mechanic onboarding and verification
- User acquisition and behavior change
- Initial capital for platform development
