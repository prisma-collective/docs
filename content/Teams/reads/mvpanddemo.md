## Problem
Many students in Nigeria and Africa face high exam failure rates and cannot access affordable study tools. Most online prep platforms are paid and offer no real incentives to keep learners motivated, while many students are excluded because they lack smartphones or stable internet. Certificates from most platforms are not verifiable, limiting trust and opportunities. Across many Nigerian communities, students struggle with three connected barriers: low motivation to study consistently, high cost of exam registration and preparation, and limited access to reliable digital learning tools especially offline. Even when students are willing to learn, the system offers no direct incentive for daily study habits, no easy verification of learning outcomes, and no simple way to convert academic effort into financial support for exam access. Learning becomes effort without reward and many students fall out of the system not because they lack ability, but because the system does not sustain them.

## MVP

### Scope
READS transforms education into a Learn-to-Earn experience operating across four tightly linked layers:
- **Learn Layer:** Students study digital content and prepare using CBT-style practice questions
- **Test Layer:** Quizzes and CBT simulations measure real understanding, not guesswork
- **Earn Layer:** Smart contracts reward verified performance with blockchain tokens
- **Access Layer:** Tokens can be used for paying exam registration fees, accessing educational tools, and participating in the digital marketplace
- **USSD Access:** Ensures inclusion for users without smartphones or internet, removing connectivity as a barrier to education

The platform provides full CBT practice environment, verifiable on-chain academic certificates, integrated exam-fee payment support, and digital learning marketplace, creating a closed educational economy where effort, achievement, certification, and access to exams are structurally connected.

### Cardano Integration
READS leverages Cardano blockchain as a behavior-shaping infrastructure that converts educational discipline into economic value. Smart contracts reward verified performance with $READS tokens (100 Billion total supply on Cardano Native) distributed across learn-to-earn rewards, ecosystem development, partnerships, team, and liquidity reserves. The blockchain provides trust, auditability, and reward transparency—linking academic effort directly to exam access through verifiable on-chain academic certificates that become portable digital assets. This extends education access through offline USSD inclusion and directly addresses student dropout caused by exam fees, inconsistent study habits, lack of verifiable academic micro-credentials, and digital exclusion in low-connectivity regions—creating a future where students earn their way into opportunity, education becomes self-reinforcing, and learning transforms from cost burden into productive pathway.
