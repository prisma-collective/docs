# Solution Genesis

## Problem
Along high-traffic corridors outside central Abuja, transport enforcement practices have increasingly led to harassment, vehicle damage, wrongful impoundment, and violent confrontations affecting drivers, passengers, and bystanders. The emerging challenge is the breakdown of trust and safety between drivers, passengers, and authorities, which undermines compliance and smooth mobility. Even small issues like unclear documentation or difficult verification processes significantly erode people's experiences and confidence in the system, creating a cycle of fear and inefficiency on the roads.

## MVP

### Scope
Autofy provides a transparent, fair, and technology-driven system that:
- Enables easy vehicle documentation and verification
- Creates clear compliance pathways for drivers
- Provides transparent enforcement tracking
- Builds trust between drivers, passengers, and authorities
- Ensures road safety through accountable processes

### Cardano Integration
Cardano-powered Autofy creates an immutable record of vehicle documentation, compliance status, and enforcement actions, ensuring transparency and accountability. The blockchain layer prevents tampering with records, provides verifiable proof of compliance, and protects all parties through transparent, auditable transactions—making road operations accountable, safer, and more human-centered while ensuring compliance without conflict.
