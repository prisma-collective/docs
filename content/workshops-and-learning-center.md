# 📘 Workshops

## 🧭 Overview

Workshops are the core learning sessions for the Abuja Hub. They are designed to help teams improve problem framing, documentation quality, and technical execution as they move from exploration to working prototypes.

Each workshop builds on the previous one, and all materials are tied directly to the session recordings for easy reference.

## 🧱 New to Cardano or Blockchain?

If you are new to blockchain or Cardano, please go through the following resource before you start building.

👉 **Blockchain 101 and Cardano Testnet Wallet Creation** with Simon Sällström, CEO of DirectEd Development

This session covers the basics of blockchain, an introduction to Cardano, and a step-by-step walkthrough of creating a Cardano testnet wallet.
