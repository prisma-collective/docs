# Grounding & Alignment

## Purpose

To translate understanding into shared direction.

This process anchors teams in intent, scope, and feasibility before significant technical effort is applied.

## What Happens

Teams:

- Refine and stress-test problem statements
- Surface assumptions and constraints
- Align internally on roles, priorities, and decision-making

The goal is **directional coherence, not polish**.

## Implementation Approach

- **Weekly team check-ins** to validate direction and surface blockers
- **Facilitated workshops** (Tuesdays and Thursdays) to deepen alignment
- **Ongoing Q&A sessions** to prevent drift

## Outcomes

Teams demonstrate:

- A stable, defensible problem framing
- Clear articulation of intended impact
- Readiness to build with purpose

This process is evidenced through check-in notes, workshop recordings, and alignment artifacts.
