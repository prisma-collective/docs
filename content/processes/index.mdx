# Processes & Methodologies

Welcome to the Next Trend Hub processes documentation. This section covers our methodologies, hackathon guidelines, evaluation frameworks, and templates for teams and facilitators.

## CATS Hackathon 2024-2025

Next Trend Hub is a local organizing partner for the **CATS Hackathon**, running from November 1, 2024 to January 9, 2025. This hackathon follows a unique **"Build What Matters"** approach, emphasizing community-driven problem discovery through action-learning.
