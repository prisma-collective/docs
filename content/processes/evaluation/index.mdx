# Evaluation & Selection

Teams are evaluated at **two levels**: 
- **Local** (Jan 6, 2025) by Next Trend Hub
- **Continental** (Jan 9, 2025) by CATS/WADA

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What Judges Look For |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| **Ground Truth Quality** | 25% | Voice notes mandatory, diverse community perspectives, depth of research |
| **Insight Formulation** | 15% | Pattern recognition, root cause analysis, clear themes |
| **Hypothesis Clarity** | 10% | Specific problem statement, testable hypothesis |
| **Opportunity Definition** | 10% | Multiple solutions explored, feasibility analysis |
| **Solution Quality** | 25% | Functional prototype, addresses validated problem |
| **Impact Potential** | 15% | Scale, significance, sustainability |

**Total: 100 points**

## What We Evaluate

✅ **Your complete action-learning journey**, not just the demo  
✅ Evidence of community research and validation  
✅ Quality of documentation on team pages  

❌ Not just how polished the presentation looks  
❌ Not just technology complexity  

## Presentation Format

- **10 minutes**: Present your journey + solution
- **5 minutes**: Q&A with judges
- **Required**: Links to all 7 team documentation pages

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping voice notes (automatic disqualification)
- Weak or superficial community research
- Building before validating the problem
- Poor documentation on team pages
- Missing mandatory quadrants
