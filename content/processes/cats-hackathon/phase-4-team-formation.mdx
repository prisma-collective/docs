# Phase 4: Team Formation & Concept Development

**Dates:** November 23 - December 5, 2024 (2 weeks)  
**Focus:** Form teams, combine research, complete Insight & Hypothesis quadrants

## Team Formation (Nov 23-25)

### Team Requirements
- **Maximum 5 members per team**
- Members with overlapping problem interests
- Diverse skill sets (research, design, tech, community engagement)
- Must register as official team by Nov 25

### How to Form Teams
1. Connect with participants who researched similar problems
2. Review each other's Ground Truth findings
3. Combine voice notes and research data
4. Agree on one problem area to focus on
5. Register team name with Next Trend Hub

## Quadrant 2: Formulate Insight (Nov 23 - Nov 29)

**Team Activity:** Analyze combined research data

### Tasks
- Pool all voice notes and interview data
- Identify recurring themes and patterns
- Conduct root cause analysis
- Determine "superior interests" (what the community really needs)
- Document insights on team page

**Deliverable:** Complete `/team/[your-team]/formulate-insight` page

[Insight Guide →](/processes/action-learning/formulate-insight-guide)

## Quadrant 3: Formulate Hypothesis (Nov 30 - Dec 5)

**Team Activity:** Write clear problem statement

### Tasks
- Draft hypothesis in format: "We believe that [target audience] experiences [problem] because [root cause]. If we [proposed solution approach], then [expected outcome]."
- Define success criteria
- Identify assumptions to test
- Get mentor feedback during check-ins
- Document hypothesis on team page

**Deliverable:** Complete `/team/[your-team]/formulate-hypothesis` page

[Hypothesis Guide →](/processes/action-learning/formulate-hypothesis-guide)

## Weekly Check-ins

Continue attending:
- **Wednesday 6:00 PM WAT** - Team progress updates
- **Saturday 1:00 PM WAT** - Problem-solving sessions

Now reporting as teams, not individuals.

## What You Should Have by Dec 5

✅ Registered team of 3-5 members  
✅ Combined Ground Truth data  
✅ Key insights documented  
✅ Clear hypothesis statement  
✅ Team pages updated (Ground Truth, Insight, Hypothesis)
