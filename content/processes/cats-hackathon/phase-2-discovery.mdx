# Phase 2: Community Problem Discovery

**Dates:** November 8-21, 2024 (2 weeks)  
**Focus:** Ground Truth collection - Quadrant 1 of action-learning

## What Happens

During these two weeks, individual participants:
- Conduct community interviews (minimum 10-15 people)
- Record **voice notes** of all conversations (mandatory)
- Observe community problems in real settings
- Document findings in weekly reports
- Attend check-ins to share progress

## Weekly Check-ins

- **Wednesday 6:00 PM WAT** - Progress updates
- **Saturday 1:00 PM WAT** - Deep dives and troubleshooting

[View check-in schedule →](/processes/cats-hackathon/weekly-checkins)

## Key Activities

### 1. Community Interviews
- Talk to diverse community members (different ages, occupations, neighborhoods)
- Ask open-ended questions about daily challenges
- **Record voice notes** for every interview
- Take detailed observation notes

### 2. Problem Areas to Explore
- Waste management
- Transportation and mobility
- Market efficiency
- Education access
- Healthcare access
- Financial services
- Energy/electricity
- Water access
- Community safety
- Small business challenges

### 3. Documentation
Each participant maintains:
- Voice note collection (uploaded to cloud storage)
- Interview summaries
- Observation notes
- Weekly progress reports

## What You Should Have by Nov 21

✅ 10-15 recorded community interviews (voice notes)  
✅ Clear patterns of recurring problems  
✅ 2-3 specific problem areas identified  
✅ Rich qualitative data from the community  
✅ Weekly reports submitted
