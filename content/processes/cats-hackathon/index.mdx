# CATS Hackathon 2024-2025

Next Trend Hub serves as the **local organizing partner** for the **CATS Hackathon** in Lagos, Nigeria. This innovative hackathon runs from **November 1, 2024** to **January 9, 2025**, bringing together 13 teams to build solutions that truly matter to their communities.

## Program Philosophy: "Build What Matters"

Unlike traditional hackathons with predefined tracks and problem statements, the CATS Hackathon follows a **regenerative approach** centered on community-driven discovery through **action-learning**. Teams identify real problems by engaging directly with their communities, ensuring solutions address genuine needs.

[Learn more about our philosophy →](/processes/cats-hackathon/build-what-matters)

## Three In-Person Sessions

### Session 1: Idea Shaping (November 22)
**Focus:** Review community problems, validate ideas, refine problem statements

### Session 2: Prototyping Workshop (December 6)
**Focus:** Build prototypes, receive mentor feedback on Cardano integration feasibility

### Session 3: Final Hack Day (December 13)
**Focus:** Present final MVPs, official project submission, local winner selection

## Prize Structure

### Local Prize Pool: 1,000 ADA per Hub

**For hubs with fewer than 150 participants (< 15 teams):**
- 🥇 **1st Place:** 500 ADA
- 🥈 **2nd Place:** 300 ADA
- 🥉 **3rd Place:** 200 ADA

**For hubs with 150+ participants (≥ 15 teams):**
- 🥇 **1st Place:** 500 ADA
- 🥈 **2nd Place:** 300 ADA
- 🥉 **3rd Place:** 200 ADA
- 🏅 **4th Place:** 200 ADA

### Continental Grand Prize

- **Additional ADA rewards**
- **Sponsored trip to Kenya** for top 5 teams
- Selected from all participating African countries by **January 9, 2025**
