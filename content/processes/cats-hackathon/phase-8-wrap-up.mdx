# Phase 8: Wrap-up & Continental Selection

**Dates:** December 14, 2024 - January 9, 2025  
**Focus:** Continental evaluation, final results, follow-up activities

## What Happens

After the December 13 local presentations and winner announcement, there's a wrap-up period where:

1. **All teams** can refine their documentation and prototypes
2. **Local winners** (Top 3-4 from Next Trend Hub) advance to continental evaluation
3. **CATS/WADA** reviews submissions from all hubs across Africa
4. **Top 5 continental winners** announced January 9

## Timeline

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| Dec 13 | Local winners announced (Next Trend Hub) |
| Dec 14-31 | Teams can update documentation and prototypes |
| Jan 1-8 | CATS/WADA continental judging panel reviews submissions |
| **Jan 6** | **Deadline for final updates** |
| **Jan 9** | **Continental Top 5 announced** |

## For All Teams (Dec 14 onwards)

### Optional Improvements
- Polish your team documentation pages
- Add more detail to Final Solution page
- Include user feedback if you tested with community
- Update Journey page with post-hackathon reflections
- Fix bugs in your prototype

### Learning & Growth
- Review judge feedback from Dec 13
- Identify what you'd do differently
- Connect with other teams
- Continue building if you're passionate about the solution

## For Local Winners (Top 3-4 from Next Trend Hub)

### You're in the Continental Pool!

Your submission now competes with Top 3-4 teams from **all CATS Hackathon hubs** across Africa for the **Top 5 continental prizes**.

### Continental Judging
**Panel:** CATS/WADA leadership + external expert judges  
**Criteria:** Same as local evaluation (6 criteria, 100 points)  
**Focus:** Best examples of "Build What Matters" across entire hackathon

### What Judges Review
- All 7 team documentation pages
- Voice notes and Ground Truth quality
- Complete action-learning journey
- Prototype functionality and fit
- Impact potential and sustainability

## Continental Prize Pool

**Top 5 Teams Across All Hubs:**
- Additional ADA prizes (amounts announced by CATS)
- **Trip to Kenya** for final showcase event
- Potential follow-up funding opportunities
- Connection to Cardano ecosystem builders
- Media coverage and recognition

## January 9 Announcement

**Continental winners announced via:**
- CATS official channels
- Next Trend Hub communication
- Individual team notifications
- Public celebration event (virtual or in-person)

## Post-Hackathon Opportunities

Whether you win locally, continentally, or neither:

### Continue Building
- Many winning solutions start as hackathon projects
- Next Trend Hub can provide ongoing mentorship
- Apply for grants (Catalyst, WADA, others)

### Join the Community
- Connect with CATS Hackathon alumni network
- Participate in Cardano ecosystem events
- Mentor future hackathon participants

### Leverage Your Learning
- Action-learning methodology applies beyond hackathons
- Voice notes and community research skills valuable for any venture
- Problem validation process useful for careers in product, design, research

## Final Submission Deadline: January 6, 2025

**Last chance to update:**
- Team documentation pages
- Prototype URL
- Final Solution details
- Journey reflections

**After Jan 6:** Submissions frozen for continental judging.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can non-winners continue working on their solutions?**  
A: Absolutely! The hackathon is just the beginning. Many teams continue building beyond the competition.

**Q: What if we don't win locally?**  
A: You still gained valuable skills: action-learning, community research, problem validation, prototyping, teamwork. These skills transfer to any future venture.

**Q: Can we apply for other funding (Catalyst, etc.)?**  
A: Yes! Hackathon participation can strengthen your Catalyst proposals. You have evidence, research, and a prototype.

**Q: Will there be another CATS Hackathon?**  
A: Check with CATS/WADA for future cohorts. Next Trend Hub will announce any upcoming programs.
