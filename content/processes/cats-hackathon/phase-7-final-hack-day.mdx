# Phase 7: Final Hack Day

**Date:** December 13, 2024 (Friday)  
**Format:** In-person at Next Trend Hub  
**Duration:** Full day (9 AM - 6 PM)

## Purpose

This is your **third and final in-person session** where you'll:
- Submit official solutions
- Present to judges
- Demonstrate prototypes
- Celebrate the journey
- Local winners announced

## Event Schedule

### Morning (9 AM - 11 AM)
**Final Preparations**
- Last-minute bug fixes
- Test demos on presentation equipment
- Rehearse presentations
- Set up demo booths

### Midday (11 AM - 12 PM)
**Official Submission Deadline**
- Submit all team pages (7 pages required)
- Submit prototype URL
- Submit GitHub repository
- Submit presentation deck (optional)

### Afternoon (12 PM - 5 PM)
**Team Presentations & Judging**

Each team gets:
- **10 minutes:** Presentation
- **5 minutes:** Q&A with judges

**Presentation Structure:**
1. The Problem (2 min) - Show voice note evidence
2. Your Journey (2 min) - Ground Truth → Insight → Hypothesis → Opportunity
3. The Solution (4 min) - Live demo
4. Impact & Next Steps (2 min)
5. Q&A (5 min)

### Evening (5 PM - 6 PM)
**Winner Announcement**
- Judges deliberate
- Top 3 (possibly 4) teams announced
- Local ADA prizes awarded
- Continental finalists advance

## Submission Requirements

### 1. Team Documentation (Mandatory)
All 7 pages on your team site:
- ✅ Team Overview (`/team/[your-team]`)
- ✅ Ground Truth (`/team/[your-team]/ground-truth`)
- ✅ Formulate Insight (`/team/[your-team]/formulate-insight`)
- ✅ Formulate Hypothesis (`/team/[your-team]/formulate-hypothesis`)
- ✅ Define Opportunity (`/team/[your-team]/define-opportunity`)
- ✅ Journey (`/team/[your-team]/journey`)
- ✅ Final Solution (`/team/[your-team]/final-solution`)

### 2. Prototype (Mandatory)
- Deployed and accessible URL
- Functional demo (even if incomplete)
- Basic documentation

### 3. Voice Notes (Mandatory)
- Accessible links on Ground Truth page
- Minimum 10 recorded community interviews
- **Automatic disqualification if missing**

## Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What Judges Evaluate |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Ground Truth Quality | 25% | Voice notes, diversity of research, depth |
| Insight Formulation | 15% | Pattern recognition, root cause analysis |
| Hypothesis Clarity | 10% | Clear problem statement, testable |
| Opportunity Definition | 10% | Multiple solutions explored, justified choice |
| Solution Quality | 25% | Functional prototype, fits problem |
| Impact Potential | 15% | Scale, significance, sustainability |

## Presentation Tips

### Do:
✅ Start with a compelling voice note clip  
✅ Show real community members using your prototype  
✅ Explain your journey and pivots  
✅ Be honest about challenges  
✅ Demonstrate passion for the problem  

### Don't:
❌ Skip the action-learning journey  
❌ Use technical jargon judges won't understand  
❌ Apologize excessively for incomplete features  
❌ Go over 10 minutes  
❌ Have a backup person dominate (let multiple members speak)  

## What to Bring

✅ Laptop for demo (fully charged + charger)  
✅ Demo video (backup if live demo fails)  
✅ Presentation slides (optional but recommended)  
✅ Voice note clips ready to play  
✅ Team spirit and energy!
