### Example Entry: Week 1 - November 6, 2024

**Call Type:** Wednesday Check-in  
**Phase:** Phase 1 - Onboarding  
**Attendance:** 42 participants  
**Duration:** 52 minutes  
**Recording:** [Link shared in Telegram]

#### Key Discussion Points

**1. Orientation Recap**
- Reviewed "Build What Matters" philosophy
- Clarified the four quadrants methodology
- Emphasized importance of voice note evidence

**2. Community Research Assignment**
- Participants encouraged to start observations this week
- Reminder: Don't commit to solutions yet
- Focus on listening and documenting

**3. Voice Note Best Practices**
- Always ask permission before recording
- Explain purpose of research
- Store recordings securely
- Transcribe key quotes for documentation

#### Action Items

| Who | What | Deadline |
|-----|------|----------|
| All participants | Begin community observations | Nov 13 |
| All participants | Prepare 2-3 problem areas to discuss | Next check-in |
| Hub organizers | Share interview question template | Nov 8 |

#### Resources Shared

- [Interview Template] - Structured question guide
- [Voice Note Guidelines] - Recording best practices
- [Ground Truth Guide] - Research methodology

#### Questions Answered

**Q:** Do we need to focus on blockchain problems?  
**A:** No. Find real community problems first. Cardano integration comes later if it makes sense for your solution.

**Q:** How many people should we interview?  
**A:** Aim for at least 10-15 conversations during discovery phase. Quality over quantity.

**Q:** Can we work on existing ideas we had before?  
**A:** Only if you can validate it through fresh community research. Start from curiosity, not solutions.

#### Next Meeting

**Date:** Saturday, November 9, 1PM  
**Focus:** Initial research findings, observation techniques  
**Prep:** Bring notes on early community interactions

## How Hub Organizers Update This Page

**After each check-in call:**

1. **Take detailed notes** during the call
2. **Review recording** if needed for accuracy
3. **Format notes** using template above
4. **Add to this page** (most recent at top)
5. **Share recording link** in Telegram
6. **Announce** "Minutes published!" in community channels

**Best practices:**
- Publish within 24 hours of the call
- Be specific about action items and deadlines
- Capture key decisions accurately
- Anonymize sensitive information if needed
- Link to relevant resources mentioned
