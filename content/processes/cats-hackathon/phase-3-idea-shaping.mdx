# Phase 3: Idea Shaping & Validation

**Date:** November 22, 2024 (Friday)  
**Format:** In-person session at Next Trend Hub  
**Duration:** Full day (9 AM - 5 PM)

## Purpose

This is your **first in-person session** where you'll:
- Present your Ground Truth findings to mentors
- Receive feedback on research quality
- Validate problem areas with peers
- Prepare for team formation

## Session Structure

### Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)
**Ground Truth Presentations**
- 5-minute presentations by each participant
- Share key findings from 2 weeks of community research
- Present voice note evidence
- Highlight 2-3 most compelling problems discovered

### Afternoon (1 PM - 3 PM)
**Mentor Feedback Sessions**
- Small group discussions with mentors
- Critique of research methodology
- Suggestions for deeper investigation
- Identification of strong problem areas

### Late Afternoon (3 PM - 5 PM)
**Peer Validation & Networking**
- Gallery walk of all findings
- Identify overlapping problem interests
- Begin informal team discussions
- Prepare for Phase 4 team formation

## What to Bring

✅ Voice notes (on phone/laptop with audio)  
✅ Summary of interview findings (printed or digital)  
✅ 2-3 problem areas you're most passionate about  
✅ Questions for mentors  
✅ Open mind for collaboration  

## Evaluation Criteria (Informal)

Mentors will assess:
- **Voice notes quality** - Do you have genuine recorded interviews?
- **Depth of research** - Did you talk to diverse community members?
- **Problem clarity** - Can you articulate specific problems?
- **Evidence strength** - Is your data compelling?

## Common Feedback

- "Talk to more people from different backgrounds"
- "Your problem statement is too broad - narrow it down"
- "Great voice notes - now analyze the patterns"
- "This problem might not be big enough - explore deeper"

## Next Steps After Nov 22

**Phase 4 (Nov 23 onwards):** Form teams of up to 5 members, combine your research, and begin developing your hypothesis together.
