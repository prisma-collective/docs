# Intro to Learning Arcs
Purpose of the main **Learning Arc**: That produces a tech innovation, informed by the unique context of their place and designed from a living systems paradigm, where the experiential learning process itself is the experimentation by which they embody a potential system state and can use that collective insight to generate a precise intervention.

## Day 2: Community Day | From phase one to phase two

#### Main Themes
- Identifying elements that build and unleash potential. 
- Deep listening to people within the chosen community. 
- Collecting sense of place data

### Roles
1. Participants as researchers, 
2. Facilitators as enablers of learning, and 
3. Hub hosts as guides and containers.[^1]

#### Collaboration
Sharing information and creating a collective understanding of a good community.
