# Formulate Hypothesis: Problem Statement

## Objective
Develop an initial thesis or problem statement based on your research and derived insights.

## Building the Hypothesis

### Based on Our Research
Review:
- [Ground Truth findings](/team/TechKR/ground-truth)
- [Formulated Insights](/team/TechKR/formulate-insight)

### Key Insights that Inform Our Hypothesis
1. visibility = Survival
2. Digital platforms ignore artisans
3. overcrowded digital marketplaces discourage new talents
4. Talents need income stability, not occasional work

## Problem Statement

Thousands of skilled Nigerians both digital talents (designers, developers, marketers) and non-digital artisans (plumbers, hairstylists, tailors, electricians) struggle to find consistent work because they lack visibility, trust, and direct access to clients. Existing platforms focus mainly on digital skills, leaving non-digital skilled workers behind. As a result, many skilled individuals remain unemployed or underpaid, despite having valuable abilities.

## Hypothesis Statement

### Our Hypothesis
**We believe that** skilled talents in Nigeria
**faces** high rate of unemployment, poverty and lack of discovery
**because** they lack visibility and unified platform to showcase their skills
**and if we** provide a platform for them,
**then** talents gets opportunities to sell their skills locally and globally by rendering services to clients on the platform
**which will result in** reduced unemployment and build sustainable financial stability for individuals and the country at large

## Assumptions to Test


- Hypothesis A: A community-based offline job-matching center
- Hypothesis B: WhatsApp job broadcast lists.
- Hypothesis C: A website that connects artisans to nearby clients
- Hypothesis D: A platform that verifies skilled talents, gives them visibility, and connects them directly to paying clients, then they will get more consistent jobs, and clients will have access to affordable, trustworthy workers. That is why we are building TechKR. 


## Success Criteria

- Minimum of 1000 verified talents.

- 50 posted jobs monthly.

- At least 85% completed jobs.

- 95% Client satisfaction ratings.

- $100 Average talent monthly earnings.

- 95% Repeat hire rate.
