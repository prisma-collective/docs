# CATS Hackathon Events

**Duration:** November 1, 2025 - January 9, 2026  
**Location:** Next Trend Hub, Lagos, Nigeria  
**Participants:** 65 participants across 13 teams

## Event Overview

The CATS Hackathon combines weekly virtual check-ins with three strategic in-person milestone sessions to guide teams through action-learning methodology and solution development.

### Virtual Events

Bi-weekly check-ins every **Wednesday (6 PM WAT)** and **Saturday (1 PM WAT)** throughout the hackathon period.

**Topics covered:**
- Research progress and methodology
- Team formation and collaboration
- Hypothesis development and validation
- Build sprint coordination
- Presentation preparation

[View All Virtual Events →](/events/virtual-events/november-2025)

### In-Person Events

Three full-day milestone sessions bringing all participants together for intensive workshops, mentorship, and celebration.

| Date | Event | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Nov 22, 2025 | Idea Shaping & Validation | Research presentations, mentor feedback, team formation |
| Dec 6, 2025 | Prototyping Workshop | Solution planning, MVP scoping, build sprint prep |
| Dec 13, 2025 | Final Hack Day | Team presentations, judging, winner announcement |

[View All In-Person Events →](/events/in-person-events/idea-shaping-nov-22)

## Event Format

**Virtual Check-ins (75-90 min):**
- Progress updates from teams
- Q&A with mentors
- Methodology guidance
- Peer learning and support

**In-Person Sessions (Full Day):**
- Hands-on workshops
- One-on-one mentor feedback
- Team collaboration time
- Celebration and networking

## Key Dates

- **Nov 1, 2025:** Hackathon kickoff (virtual)
- **Nov 22, 2025:** First in-person session
- **Dec 6, 2025:** Second in-person session
- **Dec 13, 2025:** Final presentations & local winners
- **Jan 9, 2026:** Continental evaluation
