# 🏛️ In-Person Hackathon Day

**Abuja, Nigeria**

This is a one-day, in-person hackathon focused on collaboration, rapid building, and preparing teams for the wider CATS26 hackathon journey.

## 🎯 What We're Doing Today

- Work in teams to explore a real problem
- Design a practical solution
- Build an early prototype or clear concept
- Get feedback from mentors and facilitators

By the end of the day, every team should have something tangible they can refine and submit later.

## 🗓️ Day Flow

The day will move through these key stages:

- **Arrival & Registration** — Get settled and meet your fellow participants
- **Opening & Challenge Briefing** — Understand the problem space and goals
- **Team Formation** — Find your collaborators (if needed)
- **Concept Development Session** — Brainstorm and refine your solution
- **Prototyping Sprint** — Build something real
- **Mentor Check-ins** — Get guidance and feedback
- **Pitch Practice** — Share your progress in a friendly, supportive environment
- **Closing & Next Steps** — Wrap up and plan ahead

For detailed timings and execution details, visit the [Hub Info Page](https://www.notion.so/directed/DirectEd-x-CATS-Hackathon-Info-2a152c03837980949944c1e857742566)

## 🛠️ What to Focus On

- **Keep your solution simple and clear** — Complexity can come later
- **Show real progress** — Even rough work demonstrates momentum
- **Document what you build** — Screenshots, links, short clips, or notes
- **Ask for help early** — Don't wait until you're stuck

This is about learning and momentum, not perfection.

## 📜 Rules & Expectations

- Be respectful to other teams, mentors, and the space
- Work collaboratively and stay engaged
- Participate in activities and check-ins
- Keep demos honest and verifiable

## What Happens After

After the hackathon day, you'll:

1. **Refine your idea or prototype** — Polish what you started
2. **Continue building with your team** — Keep the momentum going
3. **Prepare for formal submission** — Get ready for the broader hackathon stages

We'll share clear next steps at the end of the day.
