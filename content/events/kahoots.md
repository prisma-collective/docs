# 🎮 Kahoots

Kahoots are a weekly part of the hackathon experience. Each week, we run a live Kahoot session based entirely on the workshop content covered during that same week. The goal is simple: help you review key ideas, reinforce what you've learned, and stay engaged with the program in a fun, low-pressure way.

If you attended the workshop or reviewed the shared materials, you will be well-prepared. Kahoots are not designed to trick you—they reflect exactly what has already been taught.

## 📋 How Kahoots Work

Kahoot sessions are held **once per week** and are played live during a scheduled session. Check your calendar and watch for announcements in the channel.

**What to expect:**
- You'll receive the joining link and instructions during the session itself
- All questions are directly drawn from that week's workshop content—concepts discussed, examples shown, and key takeaways
- Participation is open to all hackathon participants
- **To be eligible for prizes, you must be present during the live session**

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Join the hub call** at the scheduled time
2. **Enter the Game PIN** when the host shares it:
   - Go to the [Kahoot join page](https://kahoot.it) (or open the app)
   - Enter the PIN provided
   - Use an appropriate and identifiable username
3. **Stay until the end** — Final questions often decide the leaderboard

## 🎁 Prizes

Each weekly Kahoot comes with **cash prizes** for the top performers. At the end of every session, the top three participants are awarded as follows:

- **1st place:** $15
- **2nd place:** $10
- **3rd place:** $5

Prizes are awarded weekly, and every Kahoot is a fresh opportunity to win.

## 👑 The Leaderboard

The leaderboard showcases the winners of each week's Kahoot.

After every Kahoot session, the **top three participants** of that week are featured on the leaderboard. Their names and photos are added to the **Wall of Winners** as public recognition of their performance based on that week's content.

Each week stands on its own. Even if you didn't place in a previous Kahoot, you can still appear on the leaderboard by performing well in the next one.

👉 **[View the Abuja Wall of Fame →](https://www.notion.so/directed/Abuja-Wall-Of-Fame-2a352c0383798009a7a9c89eedb10350)**

## 📹 Past Kahoots and Recordings

If you are unable to attend a live Kahoot session, recordings will be made available here. These recordings allow you to:

- Review the questions
- See the correct answers
- Reinforce your understanding of the workshop material

**Note:** While watching recordings is useful for learning, only participants who join the live session are eligible for prizes.

## ⚠️ Important Notes

To keep things fair and smooth for everyone:

- You must join the Kahoot **live** to qualify for prizes
- Please use an **appropriate and identifiable username**
- In the case of a tie, Kahoot's built-in scoring rules are used


Kahoots are one of the easiest ways to stay sharp, test your understanding, and get rewarded for showing up and paying attention. Make it a habit to join each week.
