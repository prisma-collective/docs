# Session 2: Prototyping Workshop

**Date:** December 6, 2025 (Friday)  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM WAT

Second milestone focused on completing Define Opportunity quadrant, exploring solution options, and preparing teams for intensive build sprint.
