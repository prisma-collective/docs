# Session 3: Final Hack Day & Presentations

**Date:** December 13, 2025 (Friday)  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM WAT  
**Location:** Next Trend Hub, Lagos, Nigeria  
**Attendance:** 65 participants (13 teams)

## Event Summary

Culmination of 6 weeks of intensive work. All 13 teams present solutions to judges, showcase prototypes, and celebrate the hackathon journey. Local winners announced and prizes distributed.

### Event Highlights

- 13 team presentations (15 min each)
- Live demos and prototype showcases
- Judge deliberation and scoring
- Top 3 local winners announced
- 1,000 ADA prize distribution
- Celebration of all participants

## Event Schedule

**Morning (9 AM - 11 AM)**
- Final preparations and setup
- Demo equipment testing
- Last-minute refinements

**Submission Deadline: 11 AM**
- All 7 team documentation pages complete
- Prototypes deployed and accessible
- Voice notes uploaded and linked

**Presentations (12 PM - 5 PM)**
- 13 teams × 15 minutes each
- 10-minute presentation + 5-minute Q&A
- Judges score using evaluation rubric

**Winner Announcement (5 PM - 6 PM)**
- Judge deliberation (closed)
- Results announcement
- Prize distribution
- Closing remarks and celebration
