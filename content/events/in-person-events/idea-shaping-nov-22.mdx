# Session 1: Idea Shaping & Validation

**Date:** November 22, 2025 (Friday)  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM WAT  
**Location:** Next Trend Hub, Lagos, Nigeria  
**Attendance:** 22 participants

## Event Summary

First in-person milestone bringing participants together to present Ground Truth research, receive mentor feedback, and form teams based on shared problem interests.

### Key Highlights

- 22 participants presented community research findings
- 100+ interviews/suveys conducted across Lagos communities
- 6 problem areas identified for team clustering
- 6 teams formed by end of day

## Event Agenda

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**
- Welcome & hackathon overview
- 5-minute research presentations (all participants)
- Problem area clustering exercise

**Afternoon (1 PM - 4 PM)**
- Small group mentor feedback sessions
- Research methodology discussions
- Team formation based on shared interests
- Next phase preparation
